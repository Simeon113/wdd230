Site Name

City of Muskego Chamber

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide a way that the community of Muskego, Wisconsin, can come together and help each other and their city. It will provide resources for local buissnesses to grow, and help residents and visitors learn what the city of Muskego has to offer. It will provide a place for people to discuss the needs Muskego has and plan the best way to meet those needs.
